Classic

Abilities

The Classic is a semi-automatic pistol. It's primary fire (left-click) can fire 6.75 bullets per second. It has a fairly medium accuracy that dratically drops when moving or jumping. It can deal a lot of damage at close range but decreases at long range. It has an alt fire (right-click) that can fire 3 bullets at the same time. It has less accuracy at fires at a slower rate of 2.22 burts/sec. This alt fire is perfect for very close range fights.

Gameplay

The Classic is the most useful gun in the entire game. Everyone gets it for free at the start of every round and even though it's free it can still kill opponents. It definitely requires a lot of skill and luck to pull of a classic ace. The classic is mostly used when saving credits for other more useful guns in the next round. When playing a round with only sidearms in attacking, rushing tight areas is most optimal. This is to minimize the opponents long-range weapons effects and to deal more damage at close range with the sidearms. This, however has a low chance of success. The classic is very useful in defending rounds. Hiding at hidden corners waiting for the opponents is one way to play the classic. And when the player needs to retreat, firing at medium range still deals a decent amount of damage. In conclusion, the Classic requires skill and a little bit of luck to use.
