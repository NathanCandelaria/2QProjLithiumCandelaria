KAY/O

Profile

  • Biography: His name is pronounced like K.O. in boxing. This is retrieved from the official Valorant description of KAY/O, "KAY/O is a machine of war built for a single purpose: neutralizing radiants. His power to suppress enemy abilities cripples his opponents' capacity to fight back, securing him and his allies the ultimate edge."
  • Appearance: KAY/O appears to be a robot. His voice sounds like a robot, he's made of metal, and in his face also looks like a digital program.
  • Personality: KAY/O has less emotion than the average radiant (other characters). But he still shows some kind of human emotion because he makes jokes while on the battlefield.

Abilities

  • Zero/Point: This is KAY/O's signiture ability. KAY/O throws a knife and every enemy in a large radius around the knife will be suppressed. They will not be able to use any abilities and the detection also shows on KAY/O's screen, making it a useful information tool.
  • Flash/Drive: This skill unlike other flash skills, looks like a normal stun grenade. It can be thrown to blind enemies. KAY/O can use this twice every round.
  • Frag/Ment: This is the dealiest out of all KAY/O's kit. This is a grenade that bounces against wall and activates once it hits the ground. It will explode 4 times dealing high amounts of damage.
  • Null/CMD: This is KAY/O's ultimate skill. Not only does it suppress enemies around him for a long amount of time, it also makes him almost invincible. Like a robot, once KAY/O is downed, he will gain massive health to distract enemies. In this period, teammates can revive him.

Gameplay

  • In attacking side, KAY/O is the initiator of fights. Although he is not the one who enters. He keeps his range from the enemies, blinding them and suppressing them. During this time, duelists in the team will have the opportunity to attack the suppressed enemies.
  • When defending, KAY/O gathers information through his knife. Lineups are made to detect where the enemies are at the start of the round. Kay/O also slows down or even makes the enemies retreat with his grenade and suppression.
KAY/O Gameplay

These is a demonstration of the playstyle of KAY/O. Kay/O can be very aggressive in tight areas like this, however, KAY/O is more of a support. By suppressing his enemies, he allows his teammates to enter and eliminate the enemies one by one.
