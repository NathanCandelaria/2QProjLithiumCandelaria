Shorty

Abilities

The Shorty is a sidearm that functions as a sidearm. It can fire very fast at a rate of 3.33 rounds per second with 2 rounds. There are 15 bullets per round that has a very wide spread area. It's not very accurate and it's not very affected by moving or jumping. The damage dealt also decreases at long range drastically. Overall, this weapon is only viable at very, very close range fights.

Gameplay

The Shorty is probably the most unused weapon of all of valorant, and with reason. It can only fire 2 rounds at a time meaning that if you miss or the bullets don't kill the enemy, you're dead. The gun is very risky to use at attacking because normally the enemies are waiting with their rifles and snipers, and a shorty can't do anything at that range. However, it can shine at defensive side. Operators (long-range snipers) can use this gun as a sidearm to protect themselves if the enemies rush their position. It almost has 100% chance to kill at very close range and a player can switch to a long range weapon if needed. Another tactic being done with the shorty is hiding at small corners. The barrel of this gun is very short, it cannot be seen by the enemy. This combined with a high chance of killing at very close range, it is very deadly at defending side when camping corners.
